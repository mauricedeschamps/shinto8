List of Ryukyu Eight Shrines

Shrine Visit Etiquette
  • Bow slightly before entering the torii gate
  • Purify your hands and mouth at the temizuya (water pavilion) before approaching the main hall
  • Throw a coin into the offering box (5-yen coins are considered lucky)
  • Bow twice, clap twice, pray silently, then bow once more
  • Be respectful and quiet during your visit
  • Photography may be restricted in certain areas - look for signs
